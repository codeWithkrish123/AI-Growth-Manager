## ⚠️ Reality Check: What's Hard

### 1. **Getting Reviews on App Store**
- Need 50+ 5-star reviews to rank well
- Takes 3-6 months of steady user acquisition
- Users leave reviews only if they see ROI
- **Solution:** Focus on retention + onboarding

### 2. **Competition is Intense**
- 500+ Shopify apps in similar space
- Many have raised $1M+ funding
- Your edge: AI algorithm + better UX
- **Solution:** Build community + thought leadership

### 3. **Customer Trust is Low**
- Shopify sellers are risk-averse
- They want proven ROI before paying
- Trial conversion rates typically 2-5%
- **Solution:** Free tier + case studies + support

### 4. **Unit Economics Are Tight Early**
- At $29/month, CAC must be <$100
- Paid ads often cost $50-100 per user
- Requires scale to reach profitability
- **Solution:** Organic growth first, paid growth later

### 5. **Churn Will Kill You**
- SaaS typical churn: 5-10% monthly
- To reach 1000 users, need to acquire 1500+
- Each user replacement costs $50-100
- **Solution:** Obsess over retention from day 1
